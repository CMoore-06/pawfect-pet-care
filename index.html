Pawfect Pet Care

Pawfect Pet Care is a pet grooming/boarding/daycare business located in Billings, Missouri. We know it's fairly far out, but it allows us to have a large plot of land for your pets to play and have fun! We offer many services for your pets. We offer grooming appoitmnets for cats and dogs where they can get their nails done, get bathed, and get a haircut. You can even book your pet a full spa day! We also offer daycare services which extend to a lot more than just cats and dogs. We'll also watch after your lizards, turtles, snakes, and birds if you need us to. We do offer food and toys and beds for them but bringing your own is encouraged. If you're going on a vacation you can board your pets overnight. As with daycare we do offer this serivce to many animals, and it's encouraged that you bring your pets things from home.

Contact us as soon as you can to book your pet's grooming, daycare, or boarding!

-- FAQs --

We do typically accept walk-in groomings but we would perfer you make an appoitment. It makes it easier on everyone, because then our staff know what animal and how many they're going to be dealing with. Walk-ins also could happen when we're really busy so we may not get to you right away. Making an appoitment either online or over the phone will help us garuntee you a quick grooming!

The required vacinations are the same ones your vet recomends getting your pet when you first adopt them. The one we're mostly worried about is rabies. These vaccinations are required for both boarding and daycare.

You don't necessarily need to bring a thing! We have food, beds, and toys to last them until you get back. Although using the items they use daily can help them with anxiety and homesickness so we do encourage our clients to bring them.
