-- FAQs --

We do typically accept walk-in groomings but we would perfer you make an appoitment. It makes it easier on everyone, because then our staff know what animal and how many they're going to be dealing with. Walk-ins also could happen when we're really busy so we may not get to you right away. Making an appoitment either online or over the phone will help us garuntee you a quick grooming!

A lot of the pricing criteria is the same with daycare. Although boarding costs a little more on average to cover costs of keeping the boarding rooms clean, and any toys that you pet may destroy.

The cost of daycare really depends on how many animals you want us to look after, and for how long. They type of animal you want us to look after will also impact price. Typicall pets like dogs and cats cost about the same amount. More exotic pets, like birds, lizards, and turtles, will cost extra.
